Game Arcade

2048

2048 is a popular single-player puzzle game played on a 4x4 grid. The objective of the game is to combine tiles with matching numbers to create a tile with the number 2048. Players move tiles in four directions—up, down, left, and right—by swiping or using arrow keys on a keyboard.

Bingo

Bingo is a popular game of chance enjoyed by people of all ages around the world. Typically played in groups, the game involves a caller announcing random numbers or symbols, which players mark off on their pre-printed game cards. The objective of the game is to complete a predetermined pattern on the card, such as a straight line horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, or achieving a blackout where all numbers on the card are marked off.

Sudoku

Sudoku is a captivating logic-based puzzle game that has gained immense popularity worldwide. Played on a 9x9 grid, the game consists of filling in the grid with digits from 1 to 9, with the objective of ensuring that each row, each column, and each of the nine 3x3 subgrids (also called "boxes" or "regions") contains all of the digits from 1 to 9 without any repetition.
