How to Play Bingo

Welcome to our Bingo game! Here are the steps to play:

  1. Click the 'Start' button to begin the game.
  2. Watch the bingo caller announce a new number.
  3. Check your bingo card for the called number.
  4. If you have the called number on your card, mark it.
  5. Continue marking numbers as they are called.
  6. If you complete a row, column, or diagonal with marked numbers, click the 'Bingo!' button.
  7. Once you call Bingo, the game will check your card to confirm your win.
  8. Follow the instructions to start a new game if desired.

Enjoy playing Bingo and good luck!
